Defining the Vibe

Luxury is not monolithic. A concierge interface must mirror the physical environment of the hotel. We analyzed five distinct design themes based on key aesthetic attributes: Darkness (Intimacy), Opulence (Traditional Luxury), Minimalism (Simplicity), Organic (Natural Elements), and Tech-Forwardness. Emotional Mapping

Kyoto Zen anchors the calm/natural quadrant, while Neo-Art Deco pushes the boundaries of constructed energy.
